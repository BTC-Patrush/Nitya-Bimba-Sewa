Lend Your Skills

Volunteer & Internship Programs

Gain hands-on experience in the field of scientific social work. We offer structured programs for both dedicated volunteers and university students.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our field teams across Nepal. We are always looking for passionate individuals to assist with:

  • STEM Facilitation: Assist in running mobile science labs in rural schools.
  • Medical Support: Non-clinical support roles during community health camps.
  • Digital Training: Help teach basic computer skills to women's groups.
Apply to Volunteer

Academic Internships

Designed for university students and recent graduates in Science, Social Work, or Public Health. Gain academic credit and real-world data collection experience.

  • Research Assistants: Field data collection for biodiversity and agricultural studies.
  • Impact Analysts: Assist in drafting white papers and impact reports.
  • Duration: 3 to 6-month structured placements with mentorship.
Apply for Internship

Be Part of the Core

Official Membership

Join our organizational network. We offer distinct membership tiers for general supporters and subject-matter experts.

General Membership

For community members, local leaders, and advocates passionate about our dual mission.

  • Voting rights in annual general meetings (AGM).
  • Priority invitations to NBS seminars and science dialogues.
  • Quarterly organizational updates and impact reports.
Download Membership Form
For Experts

Vigya (Expert) Council

For PhDs, researchers, doctors, and specialists who wish to guide our methodology and publications.

  • Advise on research frameworks and baseline studies.
  • Co-author or peer-review NBS white papers.
  • Speak at national and international science dialogues.
Apply for Vigya Council
Institutional Cooperation

Partner With Us

We collaborate with local governments, global NGOs, and corporate CSR wings to execute large-scale, sustainable interventions.

Government Bodies

Coordinating with municipalities for public school integrations and community forest policies.

Global Organizations

Executing joint research grants and aligning our fieldwork with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Corporate CSR

Providing transparent, data-driven avenues for corporate social responsibility funds to make real impact.
