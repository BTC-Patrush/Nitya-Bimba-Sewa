We prioritize scientific knowledge and social welfare

Where Scientific Inquiry Meets Human Compassion.

Advancing evidence-based solutions for Nepal’s communities. Empowering youth, women, and vulnerable populations through data-driven welfare and holistic education.

Explore Our Impact Partner With Us

Nitya Bimba Sewa is dedicated to bridging the gap between rational scientific research and grassroots social service. We believe that progress is only sustainable when guided by data and fueled by empathy.

Our Strategy

4 Pillars of Impact

Addressing systemic challenges through a multidisciplinary approach combining laboratory precision with field-level execution.

STEM & Education

Demystifying science for the next generation through hands-on community labs and interactive academic workshops.

Environmental Resilience

Research-led biodiversity conservation, sustainable forestry, and empowering farmers with eco-friendly agriculture tech.

Community Wellbeing

Holistic health interventions, free medical camps, and dedicated trauma-informed mental health counseling.

Leadership & Justice

Empowering women and youth through digital literacy campaigns, vocational training, and leadership development.
