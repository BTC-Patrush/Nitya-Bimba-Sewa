Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Nitya Bimba Sewa ("NBS", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our donors, volunteers, partners, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to fulfill our organizational mission and legal obligations. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical mailing address when you donate, subscribe to our newsletter, or submit a contact/volunteer form.
  • Financial Information: If you make a donation, we collect necessary payment details (such as credit card information or digital wallet IDs). Please note that we do not store full credit card numbers on our servers; they are processed securely by our third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, eSewa, Khalti).
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device type, and website usage data collected via cookies and analytics tools to improve our website experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Nitya Bimba Sewa uses your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and accurately acknowledge your donations and provide tax-deductible receipts.
  • To send periodic updates, newsletters, and annual reports regarding our projects (only if you have opted in).
  • To evaluate and process applications for volunteering, internships, or Vigya Council membership.
  • To respond to your inquiries submitted via our contact forms.
  • To analyze website traffic to improve user experience and outreach effectiveness.

3. Donor Privacy Policy (Our Promise)

We value the trust of our financial supporters. Nitya Bimba Sewa will not sell, share, or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.

This policy applies to all information received by Nitya Bimba Sewa, both online and offline, as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications. To the extent any donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our donors’ information will only be used for purposes necessary to process the donation.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website utilizes Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for the safe transmission of all data, especially on donation and contact pages.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as partner organizations or academic institutions). Nitya Bimba Sewa is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these external websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked sites you visit.

6. Cookie Policy

We use cookies (small text files stored on your device) to enhance site navigation and analyze site usage. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some features of our website may not function optimally.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data from our records.
  • Opt-out of any future communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our emails or contacting us directly.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
