Current Projects

Initiatives actively delivering results right now.

Pillar: Science & Education

Scientific Awareness Program

Bagmati Rural Schools 1,500+ Students 2025 - Present

Objective

To demystify complex scientific theories by operating mobile, easy-to-use practical science laboratories in underfunded local schools.

Results

Conducted 45+ experimental sessions, increasing science subject enrollment by 22% in targeted municipal schools.

Pillar: Health & Welfare

Community Health & Medicine Camps

Makwanpur & Nuwakot 3,000+ Patients Q1 2026 - Present

Objective

To provide basic medical screening, essential medicines, and maternal health check-ups to rural populations lacking immediate access to hospitals.

Results

Successfully screened over 3,000 individuals, distributed free chronic illness medication, and provided mental health first aid to 200+ trauma victims.

Ongoing Projects

Multi-year programs currently in active implementation.

Pillar: Environment

Biodiversity Conservation Study

Chitwan & Parsa Local Forest Groups 2025 - 2028 (Phase 2)

Objective

To initiate comprehensive research on local herbs and flora conservation within community forests, providing data-backed strategies to prevent extinction due to climate change.

Current Progress

Phase 1 completed: Cataloged 120 indigenous species. Currently training 50+ local farmers on sustainable extraction and organic herb farming.

Pillar: Education & Leadership

Women's Digital Literacy Campaign

Kathmandu Valley Periphery 500+ Women 2026 - 2027

Objective

To bridge the gender digital divide by training women and marginalized youth in fundamental computer skills, safe online practices, and digital banking access.

Current Progress

Three training centers established. Over 200 women have already graduated from the basic module, with 40% successfully opening their first independent digital bank accounts.

Future Projects

Our strategic vision and upcoming infrastructure developments.

Local Science Park & Hub

Science Projected: 2027

Objective

To establish a small-scale, interactive Science Park and Community Lab where children and youth can safely experiment and interact with physical scientific phenomena.

Expected Results

A central hub serving 10,000+ students annually, dramatically shifting the regional education focus toward practical STEM learning.

Free Psychosocial Counseling Center

Welfare Projected: Late 2026

Objective

To build a dedicated, safe space providing free, professional psychological counseling for impoverished individuals, domestic violence survivors, and marginalized youth.

Expected Results

A permanent sanctuary facilitating 1,000+ free therapy hours yearly, destigmatizing mental health in target districts.

Eco-Friendly Agriculture Coop

Environment Projected: 2028

Objective

To establish a cooperative network connecting rural farmers to eco-friendly technologies, organic fertilizers, and direct market access without middlemen.

Expected Results

Creation of a sustainable, self-funding cooperative serving 500+ rural farmers, boosting incomes while reducing soil degradation.

Int'l Collaboration Hub

Research Projected: 2028+

Objective

To construct a central research facility in partnership with national and international experts to execute large-scale, data-driven developmental projects across Nepal.
